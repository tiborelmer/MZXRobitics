Educational Robotics

Educational robots play an increasingly important role in modern learning environments. Designed to introduce students to the basics of robotics, programming, and computer science, these educational tools make learning interactive and fun. By integrating robots into education, teachers can spark students' curiosity, develop critical thinking skills, and prepare them for future technological challenges.

Educational robots are found in primary schools, secondary schools, and even university programs. They cover a wide range of applications, from basic concepts of logic and programming to more advanced projects involving artificial intelligence and automation.

Types of Educational Robots

Simple Programmable Robots

These robots are designed for young learners and are often used in primary schools. They are easy to handle and program, with intuitive user interfaces.

Modular Robots

Modular robots allow students to build their own robots using kits with various components. They offer greater flexibility and help students understand engineering and electronics principles.

Humanoid Robots

Humanoid robots, equipped with movement capabilities and sometimes voice recognition, are used to teach more advanced concepts, including social robotics and human-machine interaction.

Accessories and Software

Educational robots are often accompanied by educational software that allows students to program them using visual or textual languages. This software can be adapted to different skill levels, from block-based programming for beginners to more advanced languages like Python or C++.

Dobot and UBTECH: Leaders in Educational Robots

Brands like Dobot and UBTECH play a key role in the innovation and democratization of educational robots. Dobot offers versatile robotic arms, suitable for STEM projects and learning programming and automation. UBTECH, on the other hand, is known for its humanoid and modular robots, which make learning robotics engaging and accessible at all levels.

Thanks to their advanced technological solutions, Dobot and UBTECH enable teachers to provide rich and immersive learning experiences, tailored to the needs of students of all ages.
